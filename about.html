About Me

Thank you for taking a moment to learn about me

Greg Tosato

Hello my name is Greg. I was originally born and raised in Canada. I moved to Australia in 2003 to marry my wonderful wife of nearly 20 years.

Before the days of the Internet, I took an interest in computer programming as a teenager, writing BASIC code on my Commodore 64.

I pursued computer studies in College, earning a 3-Year Diploma in Business with a major in Information Systems.

In 2015, I wrote and released several apps for the Android and iOS platforms, before taking a break to care for my daughter full time.

At the moment, I am upskilling with the intent of re-entering the IT industry.

Outside of work, I enjoy competing in darts tournaments at local and state level. I also enjoy playing various instruments including guitar, piano, and drums.
